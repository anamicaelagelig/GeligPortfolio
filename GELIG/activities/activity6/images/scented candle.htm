Why Luxury Strong-Scented Candles Are Worth the Investment

Luxury scented candles can enhance your home's ambiance. But are they worth the higher cost? Yes! Find out why luxury...

By Blissy Editors Beauty contributor June 13, 2024

Listen to an audio transcription of this post

A scented candle can instantly add an inviting aroma to any room in your home. And, while you could easily find a cheap one, luxury candles are a better purchase for many reasons. In fact, more and more people are realizing the benefits of high-end candles—so much so that sales of these products have rocketed in recent years!

So if you're looking for a candle to create a calming environment in your bedroom or to freshen up your living room or kitchen—luxury scented candles are the way to go. Let's explore what makes luxury scented candles a smart choice for home decor, health, and wellness!

Top Benefits of Luxury Strong-Scented Candles

Why choose these candles for your home? The following are just a few of the main benefits they provide.

Superior quality and craftsmanship

You get what you pay for when purchasing luxury scented candles—and that's a good thing! These candles are made using high-quality wax, such as:

  • Soy

  • Beeswax

  • Coconut wax

These premium types of wax offer a cleaner burn than paraffin—the wax that cheaper candles tend to have. Healthcare experts and scientists recommend choosing candles made of soy, beeswax, or similar waxes. Why? Paraffin-scented candles have been linked to some health issues.

Paraffin wax can cause health problems

A 2023 study found that paraffin-scented candles can cause headaches, shortness of breath, and other health problems. And a 2015 study found that they release formaldehyde and other volatile organic compounds (VOCs)—whether they're lit or unlit!

Luxury candles don't just have better quality ingredients that are better for your health. They also have outstanding craftsmanship using various techniques. Some are even hand-poured, giving them a beautiful and unique design.

 

Intense and lasting fragrances

Inexpensive scented candles often use synthetic perfumes with fragrances that don't last long. That means you must keep replacing them, which adds up over time!

Luxury candles use essential oils—a natural source of scents. These give off fragrances that tend to be stronger and last longer overall.

Luxurious home ambiance

Burning inexpensive candles might fill a room with a pleasant scent for a bit. But it doesn't quite have the same quality as burning luxury candles. These premium-quality candles go a long way toward creating a luxurious and comforting ambiance in your home.

Emotional and mental wellness benefits

Did you know luxury candles made with essential oils rather than synthetic scents may boost your mental and emotional well-being? Here's what science has to say about them:

  • A 2023 study on essential oils, like lavender and eucalyptus, found that they help reduce stress and have other beneficial effects on the nervous system

  • A 2024 study on lychee-scented soy candles found that they help ease stress and may help improve sleep quality

Those are just a couple of examples of the benefits that luxury scented candles can bring to your life. Their blissful aromas might also elevate your mood while filling your home with a soothing scent.
